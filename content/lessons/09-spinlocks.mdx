## What this lesson will cover

- Test-and-set spinlock: the simplest design and its fairness / cache-line-storm problems.
- Ticket lock: FIFO fairness with a counter pair; still bounces the line.
- MCS lock: per-thread queue nodes, spin on a local flag, O(1) cache traffic on handoff.
- Backoff strategies: exponential, proportional.
- When a spinlock is the right choice (short critical sections, never preempted) vs when to use a futex-backed mutex.
