## What this lesson will cover

- ThreadSanitizer (`-fsanitize=thread`): low overhead, instrumentation-based happens-before tracker.
- Helgrind / DRD (Valgrind): slower but detects lock-order violations and API misuse.
- `rr`: deterministic record-replay — the debugger for "only happens in prod" races.
- Stress testing with `stress-ng`, ChaosMesh-style random delays, `sched_setaffinity` pinning.
- Why none of these *prove* correctness: model checkers (CDSChecker, GenMC) for that.
