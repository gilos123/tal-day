## What this lesson will cover

- The read side: zero barriers, zero atomics, essentially free.
- Grace period: wait until every reader that was active when the old data was retired has finished.
- Writer side: copy, modify, publish-via-atomic-pointer-store, wait for grace period, free.
- userland RCU (liburcu) vs kernel RCU.
- When RCU doesn't fit (update-heavy or fan-in writer patterns).
